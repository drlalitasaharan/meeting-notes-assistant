# First Pilot Onboarding Email

## Subject

Your MeetIQ pilot access is ready

## Email

Hi [Name],

Thank you for trying MeetIQ.

MeetIQ helps turn meeting recordings into structured notes, including summaries, key points, decisions, risks, action items, and next steps.

For your first test, I recommend starting with a structured 5 to 20 minute meeting recording with clear audio. A client follow-up, team sync, planning discussion, or decision review works well.

How to test:

1. Sign in to MeetIQ.
2. Go to Upload.
3. Upload a meeting recording.
4. Wait for processing to complete.
5. Open the result from the Meetings page.
6. Review the notes, decisions, risks, action items, and next steps.
7. Send feedback from the Support page if anything looks incorrect or confusing.

What to check:

- Did the summary capture the main purpose of the meeting?
- Were the key points useful?
- Were decisions captured correctly?
- Were risks or blockers identified?
- Were action items and next steps clear?
- Were names, owners, or responsibilities correct?

A quick note for pilot testing: MeetIQ works best right now with short to medium structured meetings. Longer 60-minute meetings are supported on a best-effort basis and will continue to be improved based on pilot feedback.

Please avoid uploading highly sensitive recordings during early testing unless needed.

If you run into an upload issue, stuck processing, or notes quality problem, please use the Support page and include your account email, meeting title or Meeting ID, approximate recording length, and what looked wrong.

Thank you again for helping test MeetIQ.

Best,
Lalita
