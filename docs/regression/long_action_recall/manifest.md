## Success criteria

- Must-capture action recall: 85%+
- Owner accuracy: 85%+
- Deadline/timing accuracy: 80%+
- Duplicate action rate: low
- Hallucinated actions: near zero
- Actions preserved from beginning, middle, and end of meeting
